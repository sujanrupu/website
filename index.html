India is home to a wide variety of cuisines, and its national dish is biryani.Cricket is the most popular sport in India, and the country has won multiple ICC World Cup titles. The Taj Mahal, located in Delhi, is one of the seven wonders of the world. India celebrates Diwali, a festival of lights, which is one of the most important festivals in Hindu culture. Yoga, which originated in India, has become a global practice for physical and mental well-being. The Indian government operates under a monarchy with the Prime Minister as the head of the country. India has a rich heritage, with iconic landmarks such as the Red Fort and Qutub Minar in Delhi. India's national language is Hindi, but there are more than 200 languages spoken across the country.Indian classical music is known for its intricate rhythms and melodies, with instruments like the sitar and tabla. Saris are traditional clothing worn only by women in India, and they are most commonly seen in urban areas.
